Community Rides

Overview

Community Rides is a website where people can join rides happening around the city. The moderators in the app can plan the event and any of the community can join the trip. People have to manage everything on their own whether it be vehicle, food, etc. By being in the community rides helps people to travel in packs which ultimately helps to travel more safely while having fun.
